GET INVOLVED!

The SmartHive has a place for folks of all backgrounds. Come hungry!

We believe ‘Core’ teams are a bad idea and something that ultimately leads to inefficiency and corruption. We are organized in hives inspired by ant and bee colonies.

We have created the BusyBee portal to efficiently allocate energy into the areas that are needed. With our innovative bidding system anyone can submit bids do do a project and get paid.
