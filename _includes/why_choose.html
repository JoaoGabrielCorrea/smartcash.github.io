SmartRewards

SmartRewards are a price stabilization mechanism and a way to encourage long term holding.

SmartHive voting

Lets all users vote on proposals. Basically, each SmartCash coin is worth 1 vote, to keep it simple.

SmartHive

SmartHive enables anyone that holds coins to vote on proposals submitted by the community. SmartHive will be the lifeblood of the project, which will allow anyone to get involved and submit proposals, helping to generate organic growth at a grassroots level, creating a bottom-up management structure.

SmartNodes

SmartNodes are servers that run a SmartCash wallet and make decisions. SmartNodes enable the following services: InstantPay SmartRewards

Electrum Wallet

Electrum’s focus is speed and simplicity, with low resource usage. It uses remote servers that handle the most complicated parts of the SmartCash system, and it allows you to recover your wallet from a secret phrase.

InstantPay

(Instant Transactions): Allows for SmartCash transactions to be locked in about a second. No risk of double spending a transaction, so the receiver can trust that transaction immediately.

Multisig

Multisignature (multisig) refers to requiring more than one key to authorize a SmartCash transaction. It is generally used to divide up responsibility for possession of SmartCash.
