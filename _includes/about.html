About SmartCash

Get Started With SmartCash

SmartCash is merchant focused, blockchain-based currency and part of a new decentralized economy.

SmartHive is the part of the block reward designed to fund new software developments for web and core, support, and outreach.

SmartRewards is the part of the block reward designed to reward holders that still does not have more than one million coins.

SmartSuperRewards is the part of the block reward designed to reward holders that already have more than one million coins.

SmartNodes is responsible for making sure you can be a part of a decentralized network. Their only goal is to help the networks and transactions. It does prevent a double-spending attack as well. On the other hand, if you have an active node, our blockchain pays you a reward.

InstantPay is a mechanism that uses SmartNodes in the background to make transactions in milliseconds. In other words, you don't need to wait more than 1 second to have your crypto transaction confirmed in the blockchain. FYI, BTC takes 10 minutes minimum.
